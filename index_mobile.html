Benefits of Using Our Mobile Services

Convenient for you and less stressful for your pet!

Using a mobile practice is by far the most convenient way to have your pet seen by a vet. Just think of the last time you were in a waiting room waiting for the doctor, or you had to take time out of your busy schedule and then be stuck in traffic to get to the vet on time.

Also, if your cat or dog could talk, they would likely tell you they would much rather be at home. Their vital signs are proof of this. It is well known that the average heartrate of a dog or cat is significantly lower in the comfort of their own home vs a vet clinic setting.

Transparent Pricing and Compensation

All of our Vets are compensated at a fixed rate and based on their reputation. That means there is no incentive for them to deliver more services and estimates are done before the vet arrives at your door.

Avoid Germs at the Clinic

Puppies, kittens, and sick animals should avoid stressful environments when they are sick or before their immune system is fully developed. Having them seen at home is a much safer way to avoid unnecessary risk of them contracting something from the other sick animals that are in the vet clinic. Also, stressed animals are much more susceptible to these diseases which is another reason to keep them at home if you can.

Dr. Hur received both his undergraduate degree and degree in veterinary medicine from Washington State University and is an active member of the Association of Veterinary Informatics.
